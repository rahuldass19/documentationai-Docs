---
title: Can't Load My Projects
description: Fix "projects not loading" in QWED-AI.
---

## Symptoms

- Project dashboard stays empty
- "Projects failed to load" error

## Causes

- Browser cache issue
- Network connectivity problem

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh browser">
    Try reloading the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Clear your browser cache and cookies, then sign in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your network">
    Ensure you have a working internet connection.
  </Step>
</Steps>
