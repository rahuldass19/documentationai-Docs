## Overview

Structure your docs for easy navigation with pages and sections.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a new page">
    Click "Add Page" in your project sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Group pages into sections">
    Drag and drop pages to reorder or group under a section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update navigation">
    The left sidebar updates automatically as you organize pages.
  </Step>
</Steps>
